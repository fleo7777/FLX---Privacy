Privacy Policy – FLX App

Last updated: July 28, 2025

📋 Data Collected

FLX only collects non-personal data necessary for performance analysis and to improve user experience: - Gender (M/F/O) - Year of birth (provided in the user profile) - Device model (e.g., Samsung Galaxy A52) - Android version used

🚫 Data Not Collected

FLX does not collect: - Name, surname, or email address - Location data - Contacts, photos, files, or any other personal information

🧠 Use of Data

The data collected is used solely for: - Monitoring game performance - Improving compatibility across devices - Producing anonymous statistics (e.g., average score by age/gender)

🔐 Data Security and Retention

- Data is securely stored. - Data is never sold or shared with third parties, except as required by law. - Data is retained as long as necessary for the purposes described above.

📤 Third-Party Sharing

No data is shared with partners or advertising services.

📩 Your Rights (GDPR)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights: - Access your data - Rectify or delete your data - Object to data processing - Data portability

To exercise your rights, please contact us at: contact@flx-app.com
